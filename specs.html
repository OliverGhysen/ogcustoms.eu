Good to know

Durability

The handles are printed in PETG — tough, durable, and UV-resistant. However, compared to injection-molded plastic, 3D printed objects have a layered structure that makes them inherently more fragile. The handles are designed to withstand normal use on the water, including occasional crashes, but should not be dropped on hard surfaces.

Compatibility

Boards from Carved and Lieuwe are known to potentially be affected by third-party handles, as they were designed with soft, flexible handles in mind. This can lead to inserts coming loose or board cracking in extreme cases. Our handles include flexible padding to absorb shocks, but we cannot provide guarantees. Use responsibly.
