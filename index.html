About

OG Customs is the work of Oliver Ghysen — competitive big air kite rider and mechanical engineer. Every handle is designed, tested, and hand-finished in-house.

By day, Oliver works as Lead Engineer at Common Sense Robotics, building AI-powered automation for manufacturing. He holds master's degrees in Mechanical Engineering, Robotics, and Artificial Intelligence.

That same engineering mindset goes into every handle — from the flex geometry and material selection to the print process and quality control. We take pride in our craftsmanship: all edges are smoothed, we use the best materials, tape is sealed with glue. These little details make all the difference.
